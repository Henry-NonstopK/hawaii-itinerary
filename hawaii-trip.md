## Day Notes

### 6/24 Wed - Arrival Day

- Concept: No overplanning. Recover from the flight, adjust to the time zone, and enjoy the first Waikiki atmosphere.
- Suggested route: Airport -> hotel luggage drop/check-in -> Waikiki walk -> early dinner.
- Pregnancy/family note: Keep walking short. Avoid long shopping sessions on arrival day.

### 6/25 Thu - Waikiki Adjustment + Ala Moana

- Concept: Pretty nearby places only.
- Suggested route: Kapiolani Park -> Waikiki Beach -> lunch -> Ala Moana Center -> hotel rest.
- Pregnancy/family note: Put the hot midday period indoors.

### 6/26 Fri - East Coast Drive

- Concept: Scenic ocean-color day.
- Suggested route: Halona Blowhole -> Makapuu lookout -> Kailua -> Lanikai.
- Pregnancy/family note: Keep water activity short and make it mostly photos, views, and gentle walking.

### 6/27 Sat - Pearl Harbor

- Concept: Meaningful family stop plus afternoon recovery.
- Suggested route: Pearl Harbor -> lunch -> hotel rest -> Waikiki dinner.
- Booking note: USS Arizona Memorial should be reserved ahead.

### 6/28 Sun - North Shore

- Concept: Main drive day.
- Suggested route: Dole Plantation -> Haleiwa -> lunch -> turtle beach viewing -> return to Waikiki.
- Pregnancy/family note: Leave early, expect possible Sunday traffic, keep bathrooms/shade/rest stops in mind.

### 6/29 Mon - Departure

- Concept: Do not add a real sightseeing plan. Get to the airport calmly.
- Suggested route: Breakfast -> packing -> airport transfer.
- Timing note: For a 13:00 international departure, aim to be at the airport around 10:30, earlier if returning a rental car.

## Safety And Planning Defaults

- Avoid: zipline, horseback riding, UTV, rough boat rides, long hikes, and aggressive snorkeling plans.
- Prefer: morning outdoor activities, midday indoor breaks, hotel rest, short scenic stops, reliable bathrooms.
- Food caution: Mark raw fish, sushi, poke, undercooked seafood/meat, and alcohol as pregnancy caution items.
- Weather assumption for late June Honolulu: warm, strong sun, brief showers possible; plan around heat more than rain.
